GringoAchilenao

Unit conversions for a `Gringo` residing in Chile

Get personalized conversion of your clothes, height, weight, shoe size, dollar amount plus more into Chilean-units while getting useful advise on visiting Chile

As a U.S. citizen who migrated to Chile, the small idiosyncrasies proved to be some of the hardest changes to make. I recalled at first being unable to tell my shoe size in Chile, my weight in Kilos, height in meters, current season. I would call relatives or friends in the U.S. very late or early in the day unaware of time difference. Keeping track of time zones in different States can be a challenge.

I started coding simple functions at first— formula-based conversions like Height, Weight, Distance those that don’t require an outside API. After completing those I created a currency-translator. Using an asynchronous function, a fetch request is made that queries current exchange rate from a currency API, then total dollar amount entered is converted into Chilean Pesos. For time difference, current location time from Chile is fetched via geolocation of IP address then that is subtracted from U.S. time entered in form. I used three different APIs in total. Finally the last section focuses on cultural difference along with things you would simply never experience in the U.S.
